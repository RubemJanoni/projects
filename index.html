My name is Rubem Janoni, a Brazilian of Italian descent, who in 2018 decided to emigrate to Europe, for professional reasons, job opportunities, the desire to learn the English language and the warm and friendly people, I chose Ireland.
In 2019 I landed in Dublin and started my journey, since then I have been very well received, Ireland is really a very beautiful and welcoming country, I had several jobs, all very different from what I always did in Brazil.
With a technical background in Telecommunications I worked most of the time in Telecom companies, but always focused on technology, I went in search of my degree in Systems Analysis and Development, now aspiring to Frontend, I seek in Ireland an opportunity within the IT universe.
As my first project, this site will be a way to share some projects and also a bit of my history here in Ireland. Any questions, criticisms or suggestions are very welcome, please use the contact form.
